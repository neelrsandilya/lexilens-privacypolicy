LexiLens Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

What LexiLens does

LexiLens lets you highlight or select text on a page, shows an inline meaning popup, and optionally runs on-device OCR for screen selections.

Data we collect

We do not collect, store, sell, or share personal data.

  • Selected text stays in the browser. It is only sent to https://api.dictionaryapi.dev/ to fetch a definition.
  • OCR is performed locally using the bundled Tesseract engine.
  • Your saved words and enable/disable toggle are stored only in chrome.storage.local on your device.

Permissions explained

  • activeTab / scripting / <all_urls>: Needed to read the text you highlight and render the inline popup. Runs only on user action.
  • offscreen: Runs OCR in a hidden offscreen document to avoid impacting the visible page.
  • storage: Saves your saved words list and the on/off setting locally.
  • Host permission: api.dictionaryapi.dev: Used solely to fetch definitions.

Analytics

No analytics or tracking are used.

Contact

If you have questions or requests about privacy, contact the developer.
